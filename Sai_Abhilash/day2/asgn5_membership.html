HPA MEMBERSHIP

All club members are also required to join the HPA and pay a membership fee in order to play in Club Chukkas, this must be payed directly to the HPA.

CLUB CHUKKAS

At Leadenham we run Club Chukkas on a Wednesday evening at 6pm and on Saturday & Sundays at 12:30pm. There is no charge for club members playing on their own horses, non-members on their own horses will be charged a 500Rps green fee per chukka. Rates for players requiring horse hire are 1000Rps per chukka for members, and 12000Rps per chukka for non-members.
