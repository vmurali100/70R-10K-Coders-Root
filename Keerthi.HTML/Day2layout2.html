If you thought Hyderabad is only famous for its Hyderabadi Biryani then you need to think twice. If you love typical South Indian breakfast then you definitely need to visit Chutneys....a multi cuisine vegetarian restaurant in Hyderabad. Even if you are not a big fan of South Indian breakfast items like Idli and Dosa, you got to visit this place just to taste the variety of Chutneys served here, as the name of the restaurant suggests. With branches at Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, Kukatpally, Himayath Nagar, Secunderabad and Hitech city, this restaurant is fast expanding its reach due to its immense popularity among the foodies.
